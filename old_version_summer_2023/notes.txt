Quick Recap

The goal of this project is to create an AI that can
teach me how to improve my chess.

To achieve this, I will do the following:
1. Create a chess engine.
    a. pieces
    b. board
    c. game
    d. user
    e. ai
2. Ensemble Model
    a. chess player - teach model to play chess and win
    b. chess knowledge - teach model to know why
        - for each move, choose top 3 best moves + classify why
        - classes why: make space, develop pieces, capitalize on mistake, etc
    c. chess coach - teach model to improve performance
        - ai to ai
